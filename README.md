# 📊 Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview
The **Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. This project uses the **Sample Superstore Dataset** to analyze business performance and provide meaningful insights into sales, profit, quantity, orders, product performance, and regional trends.

The dashboard enables users to monitor key business metrics, compare regional sales performance, identify top-performing product categories, and make data-driven decisions using interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and revenue performance.
- Monitor important business KPIs.
- Identify the most profitable products and categories.
- Compare sales performance across different regions.
- Enable interactive data exploration using slicers.
- Support better business decision-making through visual analytics.

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- 💰 Total Sales
- 💵 Total Profit
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold
- 🛒 Total Orders

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Top Products by Sales
- Sales by Category (Pie Chart)
- Sales by Region (Column Chart)
- Interactive Slicers for:
  - Region
  - Category
  - Ship Mode
